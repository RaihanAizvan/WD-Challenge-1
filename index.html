About Me

Born in Karnal, India, I became the first woman of Indian origin to go to space. With a passion for flight and exploration, I joined NASA in 1988 and was selected for my first mission in 1997.

As an aerospace engineer and astronaut, I've dedicated my life to advancing our understanding of space and inspiring future generations.
